Features

Everything you need for musical saturation, nothing you don't.

Five Distortion Styles

Console 70s, Tape, Tube, Transformer, and Hi-Fi modes with unique sonic characters.

Drive Control

0 to +30 dB of input gain into the saturation stage for subtle warmth to heavy distortion.

Tone Shaping

Tilt EQ from dark to bright, centered at 1 kHz for quick tonal adjustments.

Mix Blend

Parallel processing with smooth dry/wet control for transparent saturation.

Punish Mode

Toggle +12 dB extra drive with harder saturation for aggressive sounds.

Waveform Display

Real-time visualization showing input and output waveforms plus transfer curve.

Output Safety

Built-in soft limiter prevents unexpected peaks and protects your ears.

Smooth Parameters

All controls feature parameter smoothing to eliminate zipper noise.

Distortion Styles

Each style is inspired by classic analog hardware with its own character.

Console 70s

Warm, mid-forward saturation inspired by classic mixing desks. Soft and musical.

Tape

Rounded highs and thicker lows with a slower saturation curve. Smooth compression feel.

Tube

Gentle 2nd-harmonic emphasis with asymmetric saturation. Warm glow character.

Transformer

Subtle low-end heft with slight high-frequency roll-off. Solid and weighted.

Hi-Fi

Clean drive with minimal coloration. Transparent saturation for modern mixes.
